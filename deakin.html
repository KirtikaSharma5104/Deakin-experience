My Deakin Experience

Introduction

Greetings! I'm an International student named Kirtika Sharma. My adventure at Deakin University began after I finished my senior school education in India. I'm now studying a Bachelor of Information Technology in Trimester 2 of my first year, and I'm excited to look back on the difficulties I've conquered, the personal development I've gone through, and the memorable experiences in Trimester 1 that have influenced my time at Deakin. Join me as I share the knowledge I've amassed thus far on my transformative path.
